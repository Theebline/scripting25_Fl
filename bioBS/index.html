Beginnings.

I grew up in a small town called Pincher Creek in the southwest of Alberta. I grew up on an acreage with academic father and a academic but creative mother. I became interested in photography at a young age. Joining my mother and father, my grandparents, and my aunts and uncles as someone who always has a camera in their hands. Growing up, movies were the big thing. We always had movie nights, and I loved diving into those different worlds. I was always so interested in how they did it, making the movie. My mom would tell you that 'The Big Picture' was my favourite movie as a kid. A movie about a guy going to film school. Figures.

Middles.

As a teenager my love grew for photography, but especially so videography. I discovered that my interests in snowboarding, skateboarding, and biketrials as well as my passion for photography and capturing the moment could be cmbined. I got my hands on my parents old Hi8 camera and I started filming my friends and myself as we would ride. Once I hit highschool, I was into video editing, and I was in deep. Spending class time as well as free time putting together videos for my friends and posting online....long before youtube.

Ends.

After highschool I had children and became a welder. Working in the trade for 10 years, but still spending my free time trying to get pictures. I no longer had the sports subjects to film, so my subjects became landscapes, spending some time capturing timelapses and cutting those together. From there I continued to try and always keepa toe in the video editing pool. In 2021 I deiscovered Blender and my new found love for modeling and being able to recreate everyday items or things from my favourite movies and tv shows. This pushed me back into my love for creating. Whether it's reproducing the Overlook Hotel rug,or trying to model a piano, I just wanted to do more and more. I decided to chase my dreams again and get my dipoma in Multimedia Porduction
